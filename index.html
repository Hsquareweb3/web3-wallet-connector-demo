How It Works

1

Install MetaMask

Download MetaMask from the official website or your browser's extension store. It's available for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

2

Click Connect

Click the "Connect Wallet" button above to initiate the connection. In a real app, this triggers a connection request to your wallet.

3

Confirm in Wallet

Your MetaMask wallet opens automatically. Review the connection request and click "Connect" to approve and authorize the app.

Security First

Your private keys never leave your wallet. You only authorize specific actions and can revoke access anytime.

Your Control

You control your wallet and its funds. No app can access your assets without explicit approval for each transaction.
